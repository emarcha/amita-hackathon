Why are we gathering you?

AMITA Health’s Innovation team believes that “Aging Well” is one of the most important issues of our time. We need solutions now for a record elderly population, youth who can make better health decisions earlier and the adults who take care of them both.

That’s why we’re organizing a competition between 10 regional universities that taps into the knowledge of physicians, industry experts, community leaders and patients. The 24 hour Design Challenge will happen from Saturday, October 15th to Sunday October 16th at the Arlington Park Race Track.

How is the challenge going to help?

All student teams will spend quality time with prominent individuals in healthcare and other local businesses, learn valuable lessons in product design and teamwork and have a chance to take home cash prizes plus bragging right for their school.

Unlike most hackathons, our goal is for the hard work to go beyond the weekend; if an idea has potential, we will invite the student team to have a role in further development.

What can you do to be involved?

Participate!

We believe that your perspective can add value to the greater work being done. While you're working on your ideas, we’re going to provide high quality support for you to bring your perspective to the problem set.

Resources have already been set aside to take advantage of valuable concepts with continued student involvement.
